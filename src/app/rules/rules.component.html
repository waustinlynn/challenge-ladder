About the Ladder
This ladder is designed to get kids playing more matches! It is a great opportunity to complete with players outside your regular group and to gain additional match play experience.
Who can play on the ladder?
The ladder is open to all players ages 10 & up. Please note all players will play full court with regular ball. All 10 & up academy players are strongly encouraged to compete on this ladder.
What is the cost to play on the ladder?
$15 per player. The $15 will help cover the website cost as well as the year end pizza party!
Challenge Ladder Rules
  • All juniors will be placed on ONE ladder.
  • Players can challenge up to four positions above them. For example, if you are #18 on the ladder, the highest player you can challenge is the #14 player. If player #18 defeats player #14, they will take their spot on the ladder and everyone else will move down one spot.
  • Scoring will be an 8-game pro set (with AD). A 7-point tiebreaker will be played at 8-8.
  • The challenger is responsible for providing a new can of balls and travel to their opponent’s home court.
  • If you receive multiple challenges, you must accept the player that challenged you FIRST before playing another match.
  • The season will run from Monday, Feb 25- Thursday, May 16th.
  • Please keep parent involvement during matches at a minimum. The goal for the kids is to gain experience outside of class and play as many matches as possible!
  • End of season pizza party will be on Friday, May 17th at 6:30pm
